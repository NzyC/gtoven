Our Menu

The menu at Golden Tandoori Oven offers a variety of Indian delights, carefully crafted to satisfy both traditional and contemporary palates. Begin your culinary journey with savory appetizers like samosas, pakoras, and kebabs, perfectly paired with tangy chutneys. Dive into the rich flavors of our main courses, which include aromatic curries, creamy lentil dishes, and fiery tandoori specialties.

Vegetarian options abound, featuring fresh produce and bold spices, while meat lovers can indulge in succulent lamb, chicken, and seafood dishes. Complement your meal with fluffy naan bread or fragrant rice, and cool off with refreshing drinks like lassis or Indian teas. Finish on a sweet note with our delightful desserts, such as creamy rice pudding or syrupy gulab jamun. With an extensive and diverse menu, Golden Tandoori Oven invites you to explore the vibrant and diverse cuisine of India, all in the heart of Goulbourn.
