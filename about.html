About Us

Golden Tandoori Oven is a family-run Indian home diner nestled in the heart of Goulbourn, at 203 Auburn Street. Steeped in the rich heritage of Indian cuisine, the restaurant is a testament to a family's dedication to preserving the flavors of their homeland. With generations of culinary expertise, the family behind Golden Tandoori Oven brings a unique blend of tradition and innovation to every dish.

At Golden Tandoori Oven, you'll experience the warmth of Indian hospitality and the tantalizing aromas of authentic spices. Each dish is prepared with the freshest ingredients and a passion for sharing the true essence of Indian cooking. From fragrant curries to sizzling tandoori specialties, every bite is an invitation to savor the vibrant flavors of India. Whether you're a connoisseur of Indian cuisine or new to its delights, Golden Tandoori Oven is a place where family, food, and culture come together to create unforgettable dining experiences.
